Our Team

Science is done by people and is a collaborative endeavor shaped by the unique skills, perspectives, and passions of those doing the science. SNAPlab is enriched every day by the contributions of our members. If you are interested in our work, considering joining us in the lab, or would simply like to know more about us or our work, please don't hesitate to contact any of us via e-mail!
