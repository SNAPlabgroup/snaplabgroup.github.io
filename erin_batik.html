Erin Batik

Graduate Student Researcher

Erin is a second-year Doctor of Audiology student at the University of Pittsburgh. She received her Bachelor of Science in Communication Sciences and Disorders with a Chronic Illness minor from James Madison University. Erin is interested in working clinically with the pediatric population, and is an R25 TRANSLATES Scholar interested in translational research. She is currently working with Hari Bharadwaj and Samantha Hauser on a project on precision diagnostics for different forms of sensorineural hearing loss. Outside of school, Erin enjoys drawing and spending time with her cats, Poppy and Pumpkin.
